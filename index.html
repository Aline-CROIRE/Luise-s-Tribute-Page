Louise Mushikiwabo

Louise Mushikiwabo is a Rwandan politician and diplomat who has been serving as the Secretary General of La Francophonie since 2019. She was born on May 22, 1961, in Kigali, Rwanda. Mushikiwabo has played a significant role in Rwandan politics, particularly in foreign affairs and international relations.

Before her role in La Francophonie, she served as Rwanda's Minister of Foreign Affairs and Cooperation from 2009 to 2018. During her tenure, she worked tirelessly to improve Rwanda's diplomatic relations and represented the country on numerous international platforms.

Her leadership in La Francophonie has been marked by efforts to promote the French language, cultural diversity, and cooperation among member states. She is known for her eloquence, diplomatic skills, and commitment to fostering collaboration between Francophone countries.
